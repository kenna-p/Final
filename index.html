What's Our Story?

Families have included Edy’s in their biggest and smallest moments since 1928. From grandiose reunions to a quiet, rainy Sunday afternoon under the covers – our flavors bring people together. Moms. Dads. Kids. Aunties. Uncles. And BFFs. You’re on our minds. We love dreaming up new ways to surprise you. Tinkering away in our kitchen – channeling our ice cream parlor days of the past and combining real, high quality ingredients. The way we see it, every scoop is a chance to get somebody to stay for dessert, and sweeten the moment.In 1928, candy maker Joseph Edy and ice cream maker William Dreyer form a sweet partnership. Together they created Edy’s and Dreyer’s Ice Cream. A long time ago in a galaxy far far away...
